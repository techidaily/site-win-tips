Steps to reproduce the bug:  
1\. Open a number of files.  
 2\. Open the Open Documents panel.  
 3\. Right-click the Open Documents panel –> Arrange by  
 4\. Activate the following options: ‘Active’, ‘Ascending Order’, ‘Auto-Arrange’.  
 5\. Click on a file in the Open Documents panel.  
Expected result: The file you have clicked in step 5 will become active and will become the topmost file in the Open Documents panel.  
Actual result: Another file will be displayed and will become the topmost file in the Open Documents panel.
